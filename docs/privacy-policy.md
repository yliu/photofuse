# PhotoFuse Privacy Policy

PhotoFuse does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

## What we access

PhotoFuse requests access to your photo library solely to let you select
photos to add to your canvas. Photos are read directly on your device and
are never uploaded anywhere.

## What we store

All compositions, images, and thumbnails you create are saved locally on
your device in the app's private Documents directory. This data is yours —
it never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

## What we don't do

- We do not collect analytics or usage data
- We do not use advertising or tracking SDKs
- We do not require an account or any personal information
- We do not connect to any server or cloud service

## Third-party services

PhotoFuse has no external dependencies and makes no network requests. Sharing
a composition uses iOS's native Share Sheet — any data handling at that point
is governed by the app you choose to share to.

## Children's privacy

PhotoFuse does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.
